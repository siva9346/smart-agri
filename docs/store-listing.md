## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Naveena Uzhavan (Smart Agri) is an all-in-one farm management app built for
Tamil Nadu farmers, helping you plan, track, and grow smarter.

🌾 CROP TRACKING
Create crop cycles for each of your fields, log daily activities, and keep a
running record of everything happening on your farm — all in one place.

💰 EXPENSE TRACKING
Record every expense against a crop cycle — seeds, fertilizer, labour, and
more — and see a clear breakdown of where your money is going, so you always
know your real cost of cultivation.

🗺️ LAND MANAGEMENT
Add and manage details of your land parcels so your records stay organized
across multiple fields.

☁️ LOCAL WEATHER
Get an accurate weather forecast for your farm's exact location (or any city
you choose), including rain probability, so you can plan spraying, irrigation,
and harvesting around the weather.

🛒 FARM SUPPLIES STORE
Browse fertilizers and farm products, add them to your cart, and place orders
directly from the app — track your purchase history anytime.

👨‍🌾 EXPERT ADVICE
Submit questions about crop symptoms and issues, and get advisory support
from agricultural experts.

📋 FOR FARM ADMINISTRATORS
A dedicated admin dashboard lets farm/dealer staff manage customers, stock,
orders, notifications, and expert advisory content.

Naveena Uzhavan is built to make day-to-day farm record-keeping simple, so
you can spend more time farming and less time on paperwork.
