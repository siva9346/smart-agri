Privacy Policy

Naveena Uzhavan (Smart Agri) · Last updated: 11 July 2026

Naveena Uzhavan ("the App", "we", "us") is a farm management application that helps farmers track crops, expenses, land, and purchase agricultural products. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Account informationName, phone number, passwordSign-in and identifying your account
Farm & crop dataLand details, crop cycles, daily records, expensesCore app functionality — crop tracking
Order dataProducts purchased, order and purchase historyFulfilling orders, order history
Enquiries & advisory requestsQuestions submitted to agricultural expertsProviding expert advice
LocationApproximate/precise device location (with your permission)Showing local weather forecasts for your farm

We do not collect this information through advertising SDKs or third-party analytics trackers — the App does not currently include any ad or analytics libraries.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core features: crop tracking, expense tracking, land management, ordering products, and expert advisory.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To show weather relevant to your farm's location.
  • To let farm administrators manage customer records, stock, and orders on your behalf.

3. Location Data

If you grant location permission, the App uses your device's GPS coordinates to fetch a local weather forecast from a third-party weather provider (OpenWeatherMap). Coordinates are sent to that provider only to retrieve weather data and are not stored on our servers beyond what is needed to serve the request. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings; the App will fall back to a manually selected city.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • OpenWeatherMap — receives location coordinates solely to return weather data.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — our backend hosting provider, which stores app data on our behalf.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Security

Data is transmitted between the App and our servers over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to backend systems is restricted to the app's operators.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account and farm data for as long as your account is active, so you can access your crop history and orders. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see our Account & Data Deletion page for instructions.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for farmers and farm administrators and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
