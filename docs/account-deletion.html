Account & Data Deletion

Naveena Uzhavan (Smart Agri) · Last updated: 11 July 2026

You can request deletion of your Naveena Uzhavan account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to sivavelmurugan8@gmail.com from the phone number or contact information registered on your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered phone number so we can locate your account.
  3. We will verify your request and delete your account within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your name, phone number, and login credentials
  • Your farm and land records
  • Your crop cycle, daily record, and expense data
  • Your order and purchase history
  • Your enquiries and advisory requests

Data We May Retain

We may retain limited transaction records (e.g. completed order records) where required for accounting or legal obligations, for a period not exceeding what is legally required, after which it is deleted.
